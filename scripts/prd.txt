# FlowFin: AI-Powered Personal Finance Management Platform

## Product Overview
FlowFin is a comprehensive personal finance management platform that leverages artificial intelligence to help users make better financial decisions. The platform combines traditional financial management tools with cutting-edge AI features to provide a seamless and intelligent financial management experience.

## Target Audience
- Individual users seeking to manage personal finances
- Small business owners managing business finances
- Freelancers and independent contractors
- Financial advisors managing client portfolios
- Users looking for AI-powered financial insights

## Core Problems Solved
1. Difficulty in tracking and managing multiple financial accounts
2. Time-consuming manual receipt and transaction entry
3. Lack of intelligent insights into spending patterns
4. Challenge in creating and maintaining realistic budgets
5. Difficulty in forecasting future financial situations
6. Complex financial goal tracking and management

## Key Features

### 1. Account Management
#### Requirements
- Support for multiple account types (checking, savings, credit, investment, cash)
- Real-time balance tracking and updates
- Multi-currency support
- Account reconciliation tools
- Integration with major banks via Plaid
- Transaction categorization and tagging

### 2. AI-Powered Features
#### Spending Insights
- Pattern recognition in spending behavior
- Anomaly detection for unusual transactions
- Category-based spending analysis
- Personalized saving recommendations

#### Smart Budgeting

- Category-specific budget suggestions
- Historical data analysis for budget optimization

#### Future Forecasting

- Income projection and trend analysis

#### Receipt Scanner
